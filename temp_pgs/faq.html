Frequently Asked Questions

What is your pricing?

Pricing is dependent upon the type and scope of the project. A non-refundable creditable engagement fee is due upon signing up for service for projects hours or less. 50% of the project total is expected to be payable in advance. The second 50% is due either at the project's end or upon agreeable terms over time not exceeding 6 months. Clients will have an opportunity to discuss their project via a free 1 hour consultation.

Why the engagement fee?

The engagement fee is to compensate in case of a cancelation of the agreement with you. The minimum fee is $65 and it is equivalent to 50% of 1-2 hours of estimated work. From there the fee is $100 (50% of 3-4 hours of estimated work) and $250 (50% of 5 hours of estimated work) respectively. Above 5 hours of estimated work the engagement fee equal to 50% of the project total. The engagement fee is credited to the project total at the end of completion. The engagement fee is charged separately from the project total.

What is your cancelation policy?

1. Plans and budgets change and it is understood. Cancelations without penalty are considered if they are made within 14 calendar days. This is considered a free look period after signing the agreement and it is a courtesy. If it is found that the “free” look period has been abused then the minimum fee will be charged.

2. Cancelations after the 14 day period will be assessed the engagement fee as noted in the schedule.

What if the project details change?

Details of the change(s) will depend on the scope and priority of such changes. Please contact to discuss on how to accommodate the changes and if needed, revised plan and scope. Note: some changes may change the overall price of the project and as such can increase or decrease.

Do you offer discounts/promotions?

Short Answer: Yes!

1. Every client has specific needs according to their budgets, therefore it is possible for some clients to enjoy some form of a discount. Examples would be students, non-profit organizations, professionals in certain sectors, and government.

2. From time to time, specific packages maybe offered or lower engagement fee. Stay tuned for a chance to get a cool website for less!
