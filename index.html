Introduction

Greetings and welcome to my personal portfolio website! My name is Justin a professonal software developer, specializing in Android.

My Journey

When I was a little boy, was always tinkering with technology. First it was mastering video games and later computers. However, was not until later in life that I started to program as a student during one of my jobs. I began my official journey at a non-profit company in Cleveland, Ohio by completing first programming class in Java. After the contract ended, decided to purse programming and development on my own. Within a few months with online resources stumbled upon CareerFoundry a remote fullstack curriculum in Ruby on Rails web development, located in Berlin, Germany. Upon completion, continued my training at Grand Circus in .Net programming with the language being C# located in Detroit, Michigan. Afterwards, obtained a developer position at a consulting company in Atlanta, Georgia where I was trained on the Andoid platform and later travelled to North Carolina for a project. Today I am looking to dive deeper into Android development and prmarily open to working within the mobile space.
