About

Introduction

Greetings and welcome to my personal portfolio website, I am Justin! As a professional software developer, my goal is to produce software, websites, and web applications that are of high value for clients anywhere in the world!

My Journey

When I was a little boy, was always tinkering with technology. First it was mastering video games and later computers. However, it was not until much later in life I began to program during one of my jobs as an IT personalle at a non-profit. Being funded by the company I began my official journey and complete my first programming test by passing my first real programming class in Java. After the contract ended, decided to purse programming and development on my own. After a few months with online resources stumbled upon CareerFoundry a remote fullstack curriculum in Ruby on Rails web development, located in Berlin, Germany. Upon graduation and moving to Detroit, continued my education by applying, getting accepted, enrolled, and graduated from Grand Circus in .Net programming in C# located in Detroit, Michigan. On demo day, recieved a medal for best group .Net project voted on by employers. Today, I am full-stack developer and a contracted code coach at Grand Circus in C# & .Net.
