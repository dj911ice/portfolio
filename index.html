About

Introduction

Greetings! I am Justin, a professional web developer utilizing Ruby on Rails for back developement. The goal is to create beautiful custom yet flexible websites and functional backend for clients that are of high value yet reasonable in price.

My Story

Growing up in the eighties and ninties with video games and computers was a fasinating time. Early on my passion for computers was great and learned on various computing technologies, ranging from Apple/Mac to various IBM compatiable models. The ninties came along and with it the internet which revolutionized the computing landscape. After attending the University of Toledo for Bachelours of Science in Pharmaceutical Sciences and during my time at Eastern Michigan University for a Masters in Health Economics, started a business in selling dance shoes online. The business as well as being director of website for the Balloom Club at the University of Michigan gave me first hand experience on how webpages worked and how they were needed to be updated. In June of 2013 started working for a small appliance firm in Ann Arbor within the web-marketing department to create online content. About a year later moved to Cleveland to perform data entry and eventually became a member of the MIS department for a well known non-profit. During my time there was thrusted into the ordinary duties of a help desk personell yet was able to partake in various meetings for the creation of the new company website. Through the generousity of the non-profit, paid for some classes at a local community college. Java was the class that cemented my interest in web and programming developement. After 18 months, was made redunant and hence went onto Career Foundry after the summer with Codecademy. Thus the beginning of my jouney as a professional web developer!
