Introduction

Greetings and welcome to my personal portfolio website, I am Justin! As a professional software developer, my goal is to produce websites and web applications that are of high value for clients anywhere in the world!

My Story

Today, I am a software developer adding a stack yet was not always the case. Prior, I was in IT as part of a five person team handling user support requests and data entry at a private non-profit in Cleveland, Ohio. Became intially curious about web development as I stumbled upon my passion which is for creating and building things from scratch. Therefore, enrolled and graduated from a remote bootcamp at CareerFoundry in Berlin, Germany where I learned not only Front-End but Ruby on Rails development. After moving to Detroit, Michigan enrolled and graduated from Grand Circus in C# .NET programming.
